h1

MyWordBook – How to Use (English explanation) MyWordBook is a personal web application designed to help users build strong English vocabulary while reading books, articles, or any English content. The website focuses on real reading habits, where users often want to save words quickly without filling long forms. To solve this, MyWordBook provides a Quick Save feature that allows users to instantly store new words with their meanings while reading. After saving a word, users can later enrich it by adding detailed explanations, example sentences, and images through the “Complete Word” option. This step-by-step approach reduces effort and makes vocabulary learning more natural and effective. All saved words are displayed on the All Words page, where users can easily review and manage their personal word collection. To improve long-term memory, MyWordBook includes a Memory Booster feature that reminds users to revise previously saved words after a few days. Users can check whether they remember a word or revisit its meaning and examples if needed. In addition, the Reading Log page helps users track their daily reading activities by recording book names, notes, and newly learned words, encouraging consistency and progress. Overall, MyWordBook acts as a smart digital word notebook that supports quick saving, gradual learning, and regular revision, helping users improve their English vocabulary in a practical and user-friendly way.
